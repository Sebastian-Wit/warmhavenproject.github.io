Warm Haven Custom Design Studio

Mix and match upgrades to build the perfect shelter!

Your monthly sponsorship is vital because it ensures continuous, high-quality care, maintenance, and real-time support for the shelter and its occupant. Unlike a one-time donation, your recurring contribution funds essential services like heating costs, specialized vet checkups, pest control, and proactive structural repairs. By choosing a monthly plan, you provide consistent, reliable warmth and safety—a true 'Warm Haven'—for a vulnerable animal, adapting to their evolving needs season after season. This initiative is not funded by any government or large organization; it relies entirely on compassionate individuals like you. Your monthly sponsorship directly translates to a stable, loving environment where rescued animals can heal, thrive, and eventually find permanent homes. Thank you for being a crucial part of this life-changing mission! Witout your ongoing support, none of this would be possible.

A Note About Monthly Support

Thank you for considering a monthly sponsorship of our animal shelters! Your ongoing support is crucial in helping us provide safe, comfortable, and loving homes for animals in need.

This is a monthly sponsorship. Funding from kind, everyday individuals is what keeps us running! Our shelters are highly customizable, but if you'd like a ready-made option, check out the Custom Theme Bundles section at the end. If you just want a basic shelter, you don’t need to choose anything.

Once you’ve made your selections, please email sebwit16@gmail.com with your choices to receive a sample image of your shelter. Further customization is possible before any purchase is made.

All purchases are moderated through GoFundMe to ensure that funds are used wisely. Every donation is a shelter for an animal in need and every monthly sponsorship helps us maintain and expand our mission.
