☀️ Eco-Power: Solar Features for Ultimate Comfort

Zero-emission power for essential warmth, safety, and a touch of festive cheer.

Integrated Solar Panel Mockup

Visualizing the seamless solar integration into the roof structure.

Passive Heating Mat

The integrated solar panel charges a small battery that powers a low-wattage heating pad, maintaining a safe, comfortable temperature during cold nights and winter storms.

Motion-Activated Safety Light

A discrete, solar-powered LED light gently illuminates the entrance, providing animals with a sense of security and deterring potential nocturnal predators.

Festive Power Hub

Small dedicated outlets for seasonal flair! Easily power low-voltage light decorations like the optional Christmas garland or custom string lights without needing external power.

All solar systems are discreetly integrated into the recycled plastic roof material, ensuring a seamless, durable, and weather-proof design.
