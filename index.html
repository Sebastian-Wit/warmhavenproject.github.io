About the Project

Every winter, countless stray cats and dogs face freezing nights with nowhere to hide — while tons of plastic waste continue to pollute our planet. The Warm Haven Project tackles both issues at once: we recycle discarded plastic into durable, insulated shelters that give animals a safe, warm place to rest.

Each shelter costs around $15 to build and provides real, measurable impact — one donation equals one animal protected through the winter.

Unique Shelter Design

Our shelters are built for resilience and comfort:

  • Recycled HDPE shell: waterproof, lightweight, and weather-resistant.
  • Double insulation: keeps the inside up to 15°C warmer than outside air.
  • Soft interior: includes a washable fleece pad made from recycled textiles.
  • Windproof door flap: blocks cold and moisture while letting light in.
  • Stackable design: modular units for colonies of strays.
