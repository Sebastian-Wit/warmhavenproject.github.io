üêæ The Mission

Every winter, countless stray cats and dogs struggle to survive freezing nights with nowhere to hide. Simultaneously, tons of plastic waste accumulate in landfills and oceans, causing environmental harm. The Warm Haven Project addresses both problems at once: turning recycled plastic into durable, insulated shelters that give animals a safe, warm place to rest.

üí≤ Every $15 donated builds one real shelter ‚Äî and saves one life.

Our goal is to provide immediate relief for animals in need while promoting sustainability and creating a scalable model that can be adopted in communities worldwide. Every winter, thousands of stray animals suffer from exposure, frostbite, and starvation. At the same time, millions of tons of plastic enter landfills and oceans, threatening ecosystems. The Warm Haven Project addresses these problems simultaneously by converting recycled HDPE plastic into insulated shelters designed to withstand extreme weather. Each shelter is approximately 60 cm x 50 cm x 50 cm, large enough for an adult cat or small dog to enter comfortably, with a removable fleece pad for added warmth. We focus on cost-efficiency to ensure that every $15 donation produces one high-quality shelter, enabling us to scale production rapidly and reach as many animals as possible. Early support allows us to secure materials in bulk, coordinate volunteer distribution teams, and implement quality checks to ensure every shelter is weatherproof and long-lasting.

üè† What Makes the Warm Haven Shelter Unique

Our shelters are carefully engineered to balance affordability, durability, and animal comfort. They are built to withstand the toughest weather while keeping animals safe, dry, and warm.

  • Recycled HDPE Shell: Lightweight, waterproof, and resistant to cracking or UV damage, sourced from recycled containers and industrial plastic
  • Double-Walled Insulation: Hollow air-trap walls lined with recycled foam and reflective film maintain internal temperatures up to 15¬∞C warmer than outside.
  • Soft Interior Fleece: Washable pads made from reclaimed textiles provide comfort and hygiene for the animals
  • Windproof Door Flap: Transparent vinyl flap blocks cold winds and rain while allowing sunlight in.
  • Stackable Modular Design: Units snap together to create colony shelters for multiple animals in high-density areas.
  • Weather-Tested Design: Rounded edges and reinforced joints resist wind lift and allow snow and rain to shed efficiently

Each shelter is designed to be easy for volunteers to assemble without special tools, allowing rapid deployment to communities in need. Quality checks ensure that every shelter meets rigorous safety and durability standards before it reaches animals.
