üêæ The Mission

Every winter, countless stray cats and dogs struggle to survive freezing nights. Simultaneously, plastic waste accumulates in landfills and oceans. The Warm Haven Project solves both problems by transforming recycled HDPE plastic into durable, insulated shelters that give animals a safe, warm place to rest.

üè† What Makes the Warm Haven Shelter Unique

Our shelters are engineered for affordability, durability, and animal comfort, built to withstand harsh weather while keeping animals safe and warm.

  • Recycled HDPE Shell: Waterproof and resistant to cracking or UV damage, sourced from recycled containers and industrial plastic.
  • Double-Walled Insulation: Hollow walls with recycled foam and reflective film maintain internal temperatures up to 15¬∞C warmer.
  • Soft Interior Fleece: Washable pads from reclaimed textiles provide comfort and hygiene.
  • Windproof Door Flap: Transparent vinyl blocks wind and rain while allowing sunlight in.
  • Stackable Modular Design: Units snap together to form colonies for multiple animals in high-density areas.
  • Weather-Tested: Rounded edges and reinforced joints resist wind lift and shed snow and rain efficiently.

Designed for volunteer assembly without tools, each shelter passes rigorous quality checks to ensure long-lasting protection.
